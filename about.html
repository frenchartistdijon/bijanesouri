Bijan Souri
January 7th 1991
Born in Upland, California
Currently Living in Los Angeles, California

Hi, my name is Bijan Souri and I'm an artist and creative based in Los Angeles, CA. My focus ranges from stylized, minimal, and abstract work across different mediums such as painting and sculpture. Most of my work is me simplifying ideas and communicating tangible, intagible, aesthetic, and spatial relationships. I draw inspiration from a variety of fine artists and historical art pieces, as well as design and architecture.
